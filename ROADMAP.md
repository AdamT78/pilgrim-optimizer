- Sowing + duty/tithe action flow.

- Expand resource transactions and piety interactions.
- Add early alms mechanics with test coverage.

- Add roads, shrines, trade routes, and pilgrimage sites.

- Add buildings, hiring, donation flow, and cardinal bonuses.

- Implement full endgame scoring and tie-break semantics.
- Validate against reference examples.

- Extend from exact search to beam search and MCTS.
- Introduce comparative quality/performance experiments.

## Phase 7: Benchmark Suite and Strategy Experiments

- Reproducible scenario sets and batch runners.
- Metrics for score quality, runtime, and stability.

- Consider minimal visualization once engine confidence is high.
